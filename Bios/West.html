About Me

  • I am a creative mind who loves exploring new designs and view points. I find value in friends, family, and art. Im at my happiest when I have a laid back day spending time with the people I love while exploring new areas.

  • Senior quote:
    None of my assignments are done but I sure am.

  • Why did you choose VCD?
    I chose VCD because I wanted to be in a creative or communication field. When I found out I could do both I knew VCD was a perfect major for me.

  • What inspired you to be a graphic designer?
    My high school art teacher inspired me to be a graphic designer. She explained to me what the program at Kent was like and thought it would be fitting for me. Without her I never would have known about graphic design being a potential career.

  • Favorite keyboard shortcut:
    Command+Z

  • Worst VCD moment:

  • Best VCD moment:

  • What inspires you the most?
    Going outside or asking friends or classmates for advice. Normally different perspectives or people inspire me with new insight.

  • Goals in 5 years:
    My goal in 5 years is to own a home, live in a city that I love, and be happy in my career.
