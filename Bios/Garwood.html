About Me

  • I am originally from Youngstown, Ohio. When I am not dedicating my life to design, I enjoy film, online shopping my soul away, and watching Vine threads on Twitter. I aspire to have a design job in a fashion/beauty related field. When I'm not busy doing school projects, I am busy being a brand representative for Glossier and Rent The Runway. I also love my three cats, watching random videos on Youtube, and my daily cold brew coffee. You probably will not see me without an iced coffee in my hand.

  • Senior quote:
    "I did my best, and my best is good enough" -Hannah Beachler

  • Why did you choose VCD?
    I wanted to challenge myself, knowing how hard the fundamental courses were.

  • What inspired you to be a graphic designer?
    Design is a platform that we, as creatives, have a voice. We can create with purpose. That to me, is more important than absolutely anything. Creating a story with your design is not just for the cool design pitch. You get to see your clients story come to life. Whether that be in a website design, logo design or simply a t-shirt design. Design for reason; for passion.
