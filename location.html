Getting Here

The BFA show will be located in the Taylor Hall Gallery at Kent State University, located at 300 Midway Dr, Kent, Ohio 44243.

Taylor Hall Gallery

The Taylor Hall Gallery is located on the second floor of Taylor Hall. The second floor is accessible through the steps on the exterior of the building or through the first floor entrances. Guests with limited mobility may use the elevator located in the first floor entrance near the May 4 Museum.
